## **Part 1: MICA Estates Management Portal**

During my first year as part of JuMCSA, I had the opportunity to support estate-related initiatives. Through that experience, I witnessed firsthand the friction points the team navigates daily—scattered communication channels, duplicated record-keeping, delayed response tracking, and difficulty surfacing key information for decision-making. These challenges often meant more manual work, slower resolutions, and lost visibility into what's happening across campus.

I built this portal to address those exact pain points. It consolidates estate operations into a unified dashboard, enabling the team to:
- Track and manage maintenance requests and vendor communications in one place
- Maintain centralized, accessible records of campus infrastructure and assets
- Monitor request timelines and resolution metrics
- Generate reports for oversight and planning

**Why It Matters:** Reduces manual coordination, speeds up request handling, creates transparent visibility for leadership, and requires minimal training.

I'd be happy to walk you through a demo.

## **Part 2: Proposal - AI Operations & Innovation Lab**

As MICA evolves into a university with expanded programs (CCE, CCC, and future offerings), there's an immediate opportunity to establish an **AI Operations & Innovation Lab**—a student-led initiative that applies AI to **three interconnected areas:**

### **1. MICA Operations & Administration**

Deploy AI to optimize how MICA runs across critical functions:
- **Admissions & student onboarding**: Automate application screening, generate personalized outreach, streamline document processing
- **Academic workflows**: AI-assisted grading rubrics, automated feedback generation for assignments, personalized study recommendations
- **Student services**: Intelligent chatbots for FAQs (admissions, finance, housing), automated scheduling for counselor appointments
- **Communications & marketing**: Auto-generate social media content, optimize email campaigns, analyze audience engagement
- **Career services**: Resume optimization tools, interview prep assistants, alumni network matching

*Real-world impact:* Free up staff time, improve student experience, make MICA more efficient—while serving as a living lab for AI in education.

### **2. AI Education for Students**

Students don't just learn AI in theory; they learn by **building and deploying it:**
- Hands-on workshops: "Building a chatbot for MICA," "Using AI for content creation," "Data analysis with AI tools"
- Mentorship from AI practitioners working on real MICA problems
- Students earn portfolio pieces by shipping actual tools that MICA uses
- Understand AI's real constraints, ethics, and practical limitations through direct experience

### **3. Personal & Student Operations**

Equip students with AI tools and literacy for their own productivity:
- Study assistants and research tools for coursework
- AI-powered project planning and workflow optimization
- Content creation tools for student publications, portfolios, and creator work
- Interview prep and career transition support

**Why This Matters for MICA:**

- **Operational efficiency**: Solves real pain points across admin, academics, and student services—just like the estates portal
- **Strategic positioning**: As a university-to-be, MICA becomes known as **AI-forward and innovation-led**—not just teaching about AI, but implementing it
- **Student differentiation**: Graduates have *practical AI implementation experience*, not just theory—a significant hiring advantage
- **Scalability**: Solutions built for core operations can be refined and expanded across the university's growing programs

**Getting Started:**

**Phase 1 (Immediate):** Identify 2–3 high-impact MICA workflows to automate with AI  
**Phase 2:** Launch student cohort to own these projects with mentorship  
**Phase 3:** Expand scope and student participation as solutions prove value  

This lab becomes a proving ground for how educational institutions can leverage AI—and positions MICA as a thought leader in that space.
